Welcome to Taiwan Social Explorer. An interactive map to explore census data spatially and temporally.

Use the control panel on the right to display different data layers. A map legend, figures, and datasets (.csv) can be accessed using the control panel on the left.

Data for this project was provided by the Taiwan Census Office (1956, 1966, 1980, and 1990 data) and the Directorate-General of Budget, Accounting, and Statistics (2000 and 2010 data).
